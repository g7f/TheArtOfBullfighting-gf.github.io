The Bullring


The corida audience is seated in the tendidos (stalls) or in the palcons (balcony), where the presidencia (president's box) is situated.


Palcos & Tendidos



Opposite are the puerta de cuadrillas, through which the matador and team arrive, and the arrastre de toros (exit for the bulls).




Before entering the ring, the matadors wait in the callejo'n (corridor) behinde barreras and burlandderos (barriers). Horses are kept in the patio de caballos and the bulls in the corrales.
